Find the best source for your research

Classification of Academic Journals aims to make research easier for you by bringing all publications in a single place.

How does it work?

We display data about journals in tables, making it easy to sort and filter for any kind of information you might be looking for.

For now, the data we have is limited to what UEFISCDI offers, but we are looking forward to expand and extract data from multiple sources.

What else is there?

We are also providing a set of useful charts, in case you are looking for statistic data about journals or categories.
